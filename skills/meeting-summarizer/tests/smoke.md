# Smoke test - meeting-summarizer

Minimal scenario to confirm the skill triggers and responds as expected.

**Example prompt:** "summarize this meeting: Ana and Bruno met about the Q3 launch.
They agreed to ship on the 15th. Bruno will prepare the release notes by Friday.
Still unsure who handles support coverage."
**Expected result:** a TL;DR, a Decisions list (ship on the 15th), an Action items
checklist (release notes — Bruno, Friday), and Open questions (support coverage).
